War Predictor

Our novel product, War Predictor does exactly what it seems to do. It predicts wars, sometimes even before they are fought. This will help save lives and allow governments to take action to prevent these wars from ever taking place.

We use machine learning to analyse datasets of the following parameters and do polynomial regression to find a pattern amongst these seemingly independent factors:

  1. Economic Stability
  2. Gap Between Rich and Poor
  3. Instability of Neighbours
  4. Military Power
  5. Immigration
  6. Military Technology
  7. Stability of the Government
  8. Diversity
  9. Public Unrest
  10. Healthcare
